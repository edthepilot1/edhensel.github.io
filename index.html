Three Key Frameworks

The mainstream of Christian thought for fifteen centuries

◐

Partial Preterism

Many biblical prophecies — including most of Matthew 24 — were fulfilled in verifiable, datable events surrounding the fall of Jerusalem in AD 70.

✝

Amillennialism

Christ reigns now at the right hand of the Father; the kingdom of God is a present, expanding reality, not a postponed future program.

✡

Non-Replacement Theology

God's covenant faithfulness to ethnic Israel endures — without requiring a separate dispensational plan that divides the people of God.
