About Ed Hensel

A Disciple of Jesus

Ed Hensel has walked with Jesus since 1979. Over the decades he has served the church as a pastor and evangelist, pouring himself into the work of making disciples and teaching the Scriptures.

Today Ed travels internationally as a speaker and house church planter, helping believers around the world rediscover the richness of the Bible's prophetic message. He describes himself simply as a disciple of Jesus — nothing more, nothing less.

His book, A Map, Not a Mystery: What the Bible Really Says About the End Times, grew out of years of study, pastoral conversations, and a deep conviction that the church deserves better than sensationalized prophecy charts. Ed believes that when we read the Bible's prophetic texts in their historical context, what we find is not confusion but clarity — not fear but hope.

When he is not writing or teaching, Ed can be found investing in the next generation of church leaders and speaking at conferences, retreats, and house church gatherings worldwide.
